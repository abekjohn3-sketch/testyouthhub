Our Youth Hub

The Youth Hub in Agona Nsaba, Ghana is a safe and inclusive space empowering young people through education, skills development, and community engagement.

Why the Youth Hub

In response to the urgent need for improved educational infrastructure and youth-focused programs, a different life partnered with local authorities to establish the Youth Hub as a center for learning, creativity, and growth.

IT & Digital Skills Center

The IT Center provides access to computers, internet services, and digital skills training to bridge the digital divide and prepare youth for modern careers.

Library & Recreation

A welcoming library and recreational spaces support learning, creativity, social interaction, and healthy development for children and young adults.

Sewing & Fashion Workshop

Our sewing workshop focuses on vocational training, fashion design, and entrepreneurship, especially empowering young women with practical skills.

Health Education

The Youth Hub offers reproductive and sexual health education in a safe, supportive environment to help young people make informed decisions.
