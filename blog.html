Headline: Building the Future, One Workstation at a Time

Date

Agona-Nsaba is about to get a major digital upgrade. We are currently in the process of transforming an empty space into a world-class ICT learning hub. This is not just about four walls and some desks; it is about empowering over 500 young people every year with coding literacy and hands-on tech training. Our roadmap is clear. We are setting up over 50 workstations with high-speed hardware and software to bridge the digital gap and boost employment readiness in our community. We want to create a space where the next generation of Ghanaian innovators can explore, create, and thrive. We cannot do this alone. We are looking for partners, sponsors, and volunteers to help us bring this flagship project to life. If you believe in the power of digital education, we invite you to join us.
